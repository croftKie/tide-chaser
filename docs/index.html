TIDE CHASER

Ready to hit the waves?

Find which waves are breaking near you.

Use Tide Chaser to keep track of your favourite breaks and be ready to go when they are.

How it works

Search for a nearby town.

Use the search bar to find a town or city near your surf spot and click search to open up the map.

Drag and drop a pin in the ocean.

Once the map opens, drag and zoom the map to your chosen surf spot and when you're ready drop the pin in the water near to the coast.

Who is it for?

Surfers/Paddleboarders

Use Tide Chaser to track wave height, interval and wind direction to make sure it's the best break for you and your board.

Kiteboarders/Windsurfers

Check out the wind speed, wind direction and weather conditions to find the perfect run on your favourite spots.

Swimmers

Keep an eye on the temperature, weather conditions and sea conditions to make sure your swim is safe and fun.
